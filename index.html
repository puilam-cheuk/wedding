Event Info

Banquet 5pm HKT

We will be performing a tea ceremony during the banquet. This is a common wedding tradition in Chinese culture when the bride and groom pay their respects and show their gratitude towards their elders by serving them tea. In turn, their families will give their blessings to the newlyweds by gifting them red envelopes.

We politely request a dress code of smart casual and up. This means you can wear a plain tee, jeans, and a nice pair of shoes, all the way up to a full tux if you want.
